    Abstract

    Aghsan initiative is a youth initiative that is concerned with young people and seeks to empower them technically and linguistically through the courses it offers in computers and the English language. Aiming to bring them to the stage of self-sufficiency in these two areas, which constitute the basic building block in light of the digital progress witnessed by our time.

    The initiative also focuses on raising environmental awareness among young people by developing curricula and accompanying activities, taking into account the environmental problems facing the world now.

    Previous Next

    Among the short-term goals of the initiative:

    1. Raising young people's awareness of the importance of computers and the English language in our daily, regular and professional lives.
    2. Enabling young people to use computers efficiently, especially Microsoft Office programs.
    3. Building the foundation stone for young people's skills in the English language.
    4. Environmental education for young people.
